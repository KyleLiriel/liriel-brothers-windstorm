# Liriel Brothers: Windstorm - Game Design

## High Concept

`Liriel Brothers: Windstorm` is a top-down heroic fantasy prototype about two young apprentice mages who misunderstand each other completely. Kyle Liriel thinks Paul is turning into a dark mage. Paul Liriel thinks Kyle is acting like a mischievous goblin. Their magic, rivalry, and affection collide during small puzzle-adventure scenes.

## Characters

Kyle Liriel is 8 years old. He is a wind mage apprentice, emotional, impulsive, and eager to prove himself. His wind magic is useful but messy.

Paul Liriel is 12 years old. He is a lightning mage apprentice, arrogant, protective, and convinced that Kyle causes trouble on purpose.

## First Playable

- Title screen introduces the name and tone.
- Top-down scene with Kyle controlled by keyboard.
- Paul follows Kyle at a short distance.
- Kyle casts a short wind gust with Space.
- Three movable objects must be blown onto glowing tiles.
- Dialogue popups show the brothers arguing and reacting.
- A win state triggers when all tiles are solved.

## Future Ideas

- Add spell upgrades: wind push, wind pull, lightning charge, storm bridge.
- Let Paul solve circuits with lightning while Kyle moves objects with wind.
- Add emotional dialogue choices that change sibling banter.
- Introduce harmless "dark mage" misunderstandings as recurring comedy.
- Add hand-drawn character sprites and animated spell effects.
- Build multiple rooms with escalating cooperative puzzles.
- Add accessibility settings for text speed, camera motion, and input remapping.
